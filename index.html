Memory Matching Game

Rules:

>The game is played by taking turns flipping over two cards at a time.
>The objective of the game is to match pairs of cards with the same picture or symbol.
>If the two cards flipped over match the player gets 2 points, the player keeps the pair of cards and gets another turn.
>If the two cards flipped over do not match the player loses 1 point, then player flips them back over and takes another turn.
>The game continues until all pairs of cards have been matched.
